OUR STORY

Funding Turkey was created to establish and manage real estate investment funds and venture capital investment funds within the framework of the Capital Markets Law and relevant legislation provisions.

Our company aims to provide investors with low risk and high return with funds established and managed on the basis of real estates with high return potential and promising ventures with its professional and experienced staff who are experts in their fields.

We came from humble roots. Through the invaluable personal experience of our Founder/CEO Cameron Deggin. Through his years of building and running Turkey’s finest real estate EcoSystem, Deggin determined the next natural

growth path for our company and our investor clients was through the tradition of real estate investment funds. Although natural for Property Turkey, it was quite a revolutionary idea. In Turkey, prior to us, only big banks and large institutions had operated substantial REIT’s and VCIF’s.

Today, Funding Turkey blazes the trail for real estate companies who aspire to compete with the titans of the investment industry. It’s only through our expertise and innovation that we have been able to stay at the top and gain an edge on larger, slower competitors.

We build on a great history stretching back into the last century. And our future in this century shines bright with growth and promise.

PROPERTY TURKEY ECOSYSTEM

PROPERTY TURKEY ECOSYSTEM One of the distinguishing factors when comparing PT Fund to similar competitors in the market is PT Fund’s Management Structure; Specifically its optimization of the Property Turkey EcoSystem. It is very common for other funds to partner with third party companies along up and down the real estate stack. Many funds do this because they have specific skills in purely managing assets. But in reality, when investing in real estate you want your managing partners to have expertise in managing assets AND investing in real estate.

Property Turkey has decades of experience as a leader in the Turkish real estate market, specifically when it comes to servicing foreign buyers. That growth and expansion positions Property Turkey as real estate experts first and

asset managers second, a unique perspective in a largely homogenous industry. It also allows for advantages in the portfolio acquisition and distribution of funds for PT Fund. Property Turkey has long had the infrastructure in place to service the needs of clients from legal, to the purchase of land, building of houses, resale properties, investment projects, vacation villas, rental and property management, and beyond. Property Turkey is a full stack real estate company

So for PT Fund, that means there is no need to outsource when it doesn’t benefit the profitability of the fund. In our view this is the most streamlined, efficient, agile and productive pipeline that exists in the Turkish market.

OUR INVESTMENT CRITERIA

Our investment criteria is quite simple, yet it has been distilled down and perfected over years of real estate deal making. We have seen it all in Turkish real estate since 2001, good deals, bad deals, scams, you name it.

The type of real estate property doesn’t matter as much to us as being to apply these three principles to any real estate transaction:

1. Buy Below Market Value - We need to get in low relative to comps and other market expectations. Our elite real estate network usually acts as the catalyst for opportunities that others can’t access.

2. Ensure Stable Income - The Turkish market is emerging onto the global scene. But, it has a reputation as being more volatile compared to developed markets. It is vital to be able to secure reliable and stable income from our assets month after month. That is why we have established our own in-house Property Management company to service all of our assets.

3. Ensure an Easy, Profitable Exit - Once again, our full-stack, all-encompassing real estate ecosystem ensures a clean exit every time. That is due to the liquidity Property Turkey’s marketplace opens up for our assets.
